01 Core Symbols 核心符号

SymbolEnglish中文PinyinWhat It Does
$d$ "dee" 微分 wēi fēn Denotes an infinitesimally small change in a variable
$\partial$ "partial" / "del" 偏微分 piān wēi fēn Partial derivative — change in one variable while others are held constant
$\dfrac{dy}{dx}$ "dee-y by dee-x" y对x的导数 y duì x de dǎo shù Derivative of y with respect to x; the rate of change
$\dfrac{\partial y}{\partial x}$ "partial y partial x" y对x的偏导数 y duì x de piān dǎo shù Partial derivative of y with respect to x
$\dfrac{d^2y}{dx^2}$ "dee-two-y by dee-x-squared" y对x的二阶导数 y duì x de èr jiē dǎo shù Second derivative; rate of change of the rate of change
$\Delta$ "delta" 差 / 增量 chā / zēng liàng A finite change or difference in a quantity
$\nabla$ "nabla" / "del" 梯度算子 tī dù suàn zǐ Gradient operator; points in direction of steepest increase
$\nabla^2$ "del squared" / "Laplacian" 拉普拉斯算子 Lā pǔ lā sī suàn zǐ Laplacian; sum of all unmixed second partial derivatives

09 Famous Equations 著名方程

EquationEnglish Name中文Pinyin
$\dfrac{dy}{dx} = ky$ Exponential growth / decay 指数增长/衰减方程 zhǐ shù zēng zhǎng / shuāi jiǎn fāng chéng
$\dfrac{d^2y}{dx^2} + \omega^2 y = 0$ Simple harmonic oscillator 简谐振动方程 jiǎn xié zhèn dòng fāng chéng
$\dfrac{\partial u}{\partial t} = k\dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2}$ Heat equation 热传导方程 rè chuán dǎo fāng chéng
$\dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial t^2} = c^2 \dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2}$ Wave equation 波动方程 bō dòng fāng chéng
$\nabla^2 u = 0$ Laplace's equation 拉普拉斯方程 Lā pǔ lā sī fāng chéng
